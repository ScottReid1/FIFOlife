Why FIFOlife

Standard Insurance
Wasn't Built for You

Most policies weren't written with FIFO workers in mind. We fix that — connecting you with advisers who understand your roster, your hazards, and your family's needs back home.

Hazardous Occupation Specialists

Mining, oil & gas, and construction carry real risk. We work with insurers who cover high-hazard roles without loading your premium through the roof.

Roster-Aware Income Protection

FIFO income isn't 9-to-5. Your IP policy should account for your roster, allowances, and the real income you'd lose if you couldn't fly back out.

Protection for the Family at Home

Your family carries the risk too. Life cover and TPD that actually pays out means they're protected whether you're on-site or at home.
